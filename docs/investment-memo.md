# Investment Memo - Maquila de Agua

**Date**: January 2025
**Round**: Seed
**Amount**: $500K USD
**Valuation**: $3M pre-money

## Executive Summary

Maquila de Agua is a water bottling maquila service targeting premium brands and private labels in Mexico. The company has secured an anchor customer with 900+ stores and is raising $500K to complete certifications and launch operations in Q2 2025.

## Investment Highlights

✅ **De-risked Launch**: Anchor customer with LOI for 900+ stores
✅ **Experienced Team**: 35+ combined years in manufacturing, retail, operations
✅ **Scalable Model**: Asset-light, replicable to other regions
✅ **Strong Unit Economics**: 35-40% gross margins, 20% target operating margin
✅ **Large TAM**: $4.2B water market in Mexico, growing 8% annually

## Market Opportunity

### Market Size
- Total addressable market (TAM): $4.2B
- Serviceable addressable market (SAM): $850M (premium + private label)
- Serviceable obtainable market (SOM Y1): $2.4M

### Market Dynamics
- Consolidation of retail driving private label growth
- Quality concerns with existing informal maquilas
- Rising consumer preference for certified premium water

## Business Model

**Revenue Streams**:
1. Per-liter bottling fees (primary)
2. Design and labeling services
3. Logistics and distribution
4. Storage and inventory management

**Customer Contracts**:
- Minimum annual commitments
- Quarterly pricing reviews
- Multi-year renewals standard

## Competitive Landscape

**Direct Competitors**:
- 10-15 small maquilas (mostly informal)
- 2-3 larger players focused on mass market

**Competitive Advantages**:
1. Premium certifications from day 1
2. Guaranteed volume (anchor)
3. Full-service offering
4. Modern equipment and processes
